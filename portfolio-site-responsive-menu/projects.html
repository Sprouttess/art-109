Bryan Ko

Profile

Bryan Ko is an Asian American digital artist from the Bay Area and will soon be graduating with a Bachelor's Fine Arts in Digital Media Art. He first started his art journey in high school, learning photography and videography. Upon his arrival to San Jose State University, he’s learned many new skills such as 3D modeling and further enhancing his skills in video editing and photoshop. His work is inspired by video games and things that he has grown up with. He creates fun and comedic artwork while focusing on self development as an artist.

My work is mostly things that have happened in my life, childhood, and things that I’m interested in. I like to have a lot of fun in my work, referencing memes or making things that make no sense. In other words, I don’t take myself or my work seriously. I’ll leave stuff in purposefully if it’s funny. The only thing I do take seriously, however, is the work process.
